About Us

Welcome to a school where every child belongs

We believe education should inspire imagination, develop resilience, and prepare learners for a changing world. Our teachers nurture each child with care and high expectations.

“Every child deserves a place to grow, discover, and shine.” — Principal Amina Yusuf

Mission

To create an inclusive, joyful, and excellence-driven learning community.

Vision

To raise confident, compassionate, and future-ready global citizens.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Creativity, and Community.

Programmes

Academic pathways built for every stage of growth

Our curriculum blends foundational literacy, numeracy, science, and the arts with practical life skills and leadership development.

Early Years

Playgroup to Kindergarten with a nurturing environment that supports curiosity, social development, and early mastery.

  • Play-based learning
  • Language and motor skills
  • Creative exploration

Primary School

Grades 1 to 6 focused on strong academics, inquiry learning, and confidence-building across all subjects.

  • English, Maths, Science
  • Digital literacy
  • Project-based learning

Junior Secondary

A bridge to senior learning with deeper study in mathematics, science, technology, humanities, and communication.

  • Advanced subjects
  • Critical thinking
  • Leadership pathways

Co-Curricular

Opportunities that build character and confidence

Our enrichment programmes help students discover talents, build friendships, and develop teamwork beyond the classroom.

Football

Training in teamwork, discipline, coordination, and sportsmanship.

Swimming

Water safety and confidence-building through structured lessons.

Athletics

Track and field activities that encourage stamina and healthy competition.

Drama

Performance and storytelling that build confidence and self-expression.

Music

Instrumental and vocal lessons that nurture creativity and rhythm.

Coding / ICT

Hands-on robotics and coding sessions that introduce digital problem solving.

Chess

Strategic play that strengthens focus, patience, and analytical thinking.
